At Vitreus Decor, we strive to deliver a quality product with a visible impact.

About Us

We are a team of entrepreneurial high school students who want to learn our way around the business world while making a difference in our community. Through our partnership with Evergreen Brickworks, Vitreus Decor is helping to plant trees in our city with the revenue from our decorative bulbs. With the assistance of Junior Achievement and Scotiabank, we will deliver to our customers a great product and hone our skills in areas such as marketing, teamwork, and leadership.

About Evergreen Brickworks

Evergreen Brick Works opened its doors in 2010. A demonstration hub where the world can experience sustainable practices that enable flourishing cities of the future. It connects citizens, business, academia and government.

We dream of flourishing cities. The backbone of our cities are the places we share and where we connect. Connecting people, natural and built worlds to city systems like housing and transit, the daily experience in our communities improves and our life in the city becomes happier and healthier

Through our award-winning suite of programs, we have actively engaged Canadians in creating and sustaining healthy urban environments in our schools, our public spaces, in housing and transit systems, and communities themselves. Flourishing cities start here – at a site, in a neighbourhood, on the ground – where spaces become great places, and have a real and lasting impact, creating a better world for all.
